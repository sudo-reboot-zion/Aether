# Aether Guest Guide

## How to Book
1. **Discover**: Ask our AI assistant to find properties matching your requirements (e.g., "Find me a 2-bedroom apartment in Paris under $100").
2. **Select Dates**: Pick your check-in and check-out dates.
3. **Pay Securely**: Your payment is securely escrowed until check-in time.

## Escrow Protection
Your money is not immediately given to the host. It is held securely in escrow. At check-in time, the payment is automatically released. This protects you from fraudulent listings. 

## Cancellation Policy
- **More than 7 days before check-in**: 100% refund.
- **3-7 days before**: 50% refund.
- **Less than 3 days**: 0% refund.

## Troubleshooting
If a host is unresponsive, wait 24 hours. If it's less than 7 days before your trip, please raise a support ticket and Aether support will intervene or refund you.
