# Aether Host Guide

## Why Host on Aether?
1. **0% Host Fees**: You keep 100% of your listed price. We don't take a cut of your earnings.
2. **AI Support**: Our AI assistant helps answer common guest questions about your property, saving you time.
3. **Secure Escrow**: You are guaranteed payment upon the guest's check-in via our automated escrow system, protecting you from chargebacks.

## How to List Your Property
1. Create an account.
2. Click "List Property".
3. Provide high-quality photos, write a detailed description, list your amenities, and set your nightly price.
4. Publish your listing, which instantly goes live on the platform!

## Receiving Payments
Payments are securely held when the guest books and instantly released to your payout method on the day of check-in.

## Managing Bookings
You can manage all your reservations from the Dashboard. You have up to 24 hours to respond to guest inquiries before they are permitted to cancel for a full refund.
