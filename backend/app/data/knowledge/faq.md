# Aether FAQ

## What is Aether?
Aether is an AI-powered property rental platform that connects property owners (hosts) with travelers (guests). We use advanced AI to assist in discovery, booking, and support, providing a seamless experience.

## What are the costs?
Unlike traditional platforms that charge 15-20% in fees, Aether charges a flat 2% fee to guests, and 0% to hosts. This lets hosts keep 100% of their earnings while guests save significantly on booking costs.

## Is my data secure?
Yes. Aether employs strict data privacy policies. We do not sell user data. Our AI models are private and do not train on your personal data.

## I have a dispute. What do I do?
Disputes can be raised within 24 hours of checking out. You just need to submit photos and a description of the issue to our support team. The team will review the claim and issue an appropriate refund automatically via our secure escrow system.
