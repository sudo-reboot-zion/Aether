# StackNStay Knowledge Base

## What is StackNStay?

StackNStay is an AI-powered property rental platform that connects property owners (hosts) with travelers (guests) seamlessly. 

### Key Features:
- **Low Fees**: We offer highly competitive platform fees compared to traditional competitors.
- **Secure Escrow**: Your payment is held securely in our system until after you check-in.
- **AI-Powered**: Smart chat agents help you find the perfect property or answer any questions you have.
- **Direct Connection**: Connect directly with hosts for your inquiries.

---

## Platform Fees

### Why So Low?

Traditional platforms charge 15-20% in fees. StackNStay leverages AI automations to handle customer service and booking logistics, keeping overhead drastically lower.

**Fee Breakdown:**
- **Guest Fee**: 2% of booking total
- **Host Fee**: 0% (hosts keep 100% of their listed price!)
- **Traditional Platforms**: 15-20% total fees

## How Booking Works

### Step-by-Step Process:

1. **Browse Properties**: Ask our AI assistant to find properties by location, price, or amenities.
2. **Select Dates**: Choose check-in and check-out dates.
3. **Book & Pay**: Payment goes into our secure escrow system.
4. **Confirmation**: Booking is confirmed immediately.
5. **Check-in**: At check-in time, payment releases to the host.
6. **Check-out**: Leave a review to help future guests!

### Escrow Protection:

Your payment is held safely until check-in. This protects both guests and hosts:

**For Guests:**
- Money held safely until check-in
- Can cancel for refund depending on the cancellation policy
- Dispute resolution if property doesn't match listing

**For Hosts:**
- Guaranteed payment after check-in
- Protected from fraudulent chargebacks
- Automatic payment release

## Cancellation Policy

### Refund Schedule:

**More than 7 days before check-in:**
- 100% refund (minus a small processing fee)

**3-7 days before check-in:**
- 50% refund

**Less than 3 days before check-in:**
- 0% refund (host keeps full amount)

**After check-in:**
- No refunds (payment already released to host)

### How to Cancel:

1. Go to your "My Bookings" page
2. Find your booking
3. Click "Cancel Booking"
4. Automated refunds are processed instantly to your payment method

## Dispute Resolution

### When to Raise a Dispute:

- Property doesn't match listing
- Serious cleanliness issues
- Safety concerns
- Host no-show or locked out
- Major amenities missing

### How Disputes Work:

1. **Guest raises dispute** (during stay or within 24 hours of check-out)
2. **Evidence submitted**: Photos, messages, descriptions
3. **Admin reviews**: Platform support team examines evidence
4. **Decision made**: Full refund, partial refund, or no refund
5. **Resolution**: Refund processed automatically if approved

## Getting Started

### For Guests:

1. **Create an Account**: Sign up directly via the StackNStay portal.
2. **Add Payment Method**: Securely add a credit or debit card.
3. **Start Booking**: Chat with our AI or browse the listings to find the perfect stay!

### For Hosts:

1. **Create an Account**: Register as a host.
2. **Create Listing**:
   - Click "List Property"
   - Upload high-quality photos
   - Set price 
   - Add amenities and description
3. **Publish**: Your listing will go live immediately.
4. **Manage Bookings**:
   - View bookings in your dashboard
   - Payments sent out after check-in
   - Respond to guest messages

## Common Issues

### "Host isn't responding"

**Solution:**
- Hosts have 24 hours to respond to inquiries.
- If no response and more than 7 days before check-in: Cancel for a full refund.
- If less than 7 days: Contact StackNStay support for assistance.

### "Property doesn't match listing"

**Solution:**
- Take photos immediately.
- Raise a dispute within 24 hours of check-out.
- Provide evidence (photos, descriptions).
- Support will review and determine a fair refund.
